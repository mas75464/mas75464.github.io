MELISSA SEGUIN

Education and Aspirations

Melissa Seguin is from Jacksonville, FL, but never once considered going to the University of Florida. Being a Bulldog was much more appealing than being a Gator, so she decided to venture a little north to Athens to attend the University of Georgia. Once at Georgia, she decided to pursue studies in Public Relations and Sport Management. While she's not quite sure what she wants to do once she graduates, she hopes to work with a football team, either at the collegiate or professional level. Currently, she volunteers with the UGA Athletic Association Event Management Department and works with the Georgia Football team in the Football Performance Nutrition Department. When Georgia wins the National Championship, just know her smoothie making skills had a small part in that monumental victory.

A Swift 10 Years

For roughly 10 years, Melissa has been a fan of Taylor Swift. Since hearing "Our Song" on the way to the playground in the 4th grade, Melissa's love of Taylor Swift has been constant. Melissa has been to concerts in Jacksonville, Orlando, Tampa, and even Baton Rouge. In addition, she's travelled to Nashville to visit places that Swift has frequented. Melissa hopes to visit Pasadena, California next year for Taylor Swift's reputation World Tour. Swift's most recent album, reputation, has quickly become Melissa's favorite, and below we have a ranking of her favorite songs on the album.
